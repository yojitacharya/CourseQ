We do not store your course content, syllabi, or any other applied study materials on our servers. 2. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND STORAGE Because the Service operates primarily on your local device, our data handling practices are as follows: Local Storage: All data you input, upload, or generate—including uploaded syllabi (PDFs/Images), parsed course schedules, AI-generated summaries, and flashcards—is stored locally on your device or within your private personal cloud storage (such as iCloud or Google Drive) linked to your device account. No Central Database: We do not maintain a central database of your academic materials. We cannot access, read, or sell your syllabi or flashcards because we do not possess them.
